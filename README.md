# Customer-Churn-Analysis-Dashboard-using-Power-BI
A Power BI dashboard project analyzing telecom customer churn to identify key churn drivers, high-risk customers, and revenue impact using interactive visualizations.

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer churn in the telecom industry using Power BI. The goal is to understand customer behavior, identify key factors contributing to churn, measure revenue impact, and provide actionable business recommendations to improve customer retention.

The analysis is based on a telecom customer dataset and is presented through an interactive, multi-page Power BI dashboard.

---

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains customer-level information including:
- Demographics (gender, senior citizen status, partner, dependents)
- Service usage (phone service, internet service, add-on services)
- Contract and billing details (contract type, payment method, paperless billing)
- Tenure, monthly charges, total charges
- Churn status (Yes/No)

---

## Dashboard Structure

### Page 1: Customer Overview
- Total customers, churned customers, and churn rate
- Churn analysis by tenure, gender, senior citizen status
- Internet service-wise churn comparison

### Page 2: Service & Contract Analysis
- Churn rate by contract type and payment method
- Average monthly charges by tenure
- Phone service subscription distribution

### Page 3: Revenue Impact
- Total, retained, and lost revenue
- Revenue contribution from churned vs retained customers
- Financial impact of customer churn

### Page 4: Churn Risk & Key Drivers
- Rule-based churn risk classification (Low, Medium, High)
- Distribution of customers by churn risk category
- Churn concentration analysis using contract type, tenure, and internet service

### Page 5: Actions & Recommendations
- High-risk customer count
- Lost revenue from high-risk churned customers
- Potential revenue that can be saved through retention
- Business-focused recommendations based on insights

---

## Key Insights
- Overall churn rate is high, with churn concentrated among month-to-month customers.
- Customers with short tenure (0â€“12 months) are more likely to churn.
- Fiber optic customers show the highest churn and revenue loss.
- Electronic check and paperless billing users have higher churn rates.
- Customers using multiple add-on services tend to stay longer.

---

## Business Recommendations
- Encourage customers to move from month-to-month to long-term contracts.
- Improve fiber optic service quality and customer support.
- Focus retention efforts on new customers during the first year.
- Promote add-on services to increase customer engagement.
- Encourage auto-payment methods over electronic checks.
